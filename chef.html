CollegeChef

Summary

CollegeChef is a mobile application made by me and three other students at Cornell University. The app is specifically tailored to college students who don't have much time and have limited ingredients. We began the design process by defining the user group, conducting user research, and creating design materials such as personas and project themes. Then, we brainstormed and sketched ideas until we had a complete wireframe on paper. Finally, we created a high fidelity prototype using Vue.js and did a round of user testing.
